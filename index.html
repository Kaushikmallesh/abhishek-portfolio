Hi, I'm Ahishek — a passionate 3D artist obsessed with creating worlds that blur the line between digital and reality.

From organic character sculpting in ZBrush to cinematic environment design, I bring visions to life using the industry's most powerful tools. My work spans game assets, film VFX, product visualization, and immersive concept art.

I specialize in the cyberpunk and sci-fi aesthetic — neon-lit cityscapes, biomechanical creatures, and holographic landscapes are my playground.
